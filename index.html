I am a mexican student in Physics at National University of Mexico. I received the degree of "PhD. Candidate" in 2024. My research focuses on Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), Magnetohydrodynamics, Plasmas and others.

I share my knowledge in my Youtube channel. I have created free tutorials and courses on scientific computation in Spanish and sometimes in English because my primary audience is from latinamerica, also I have posted my brandnew work as short or long videos. Subscribe!
